Quo Vadis

A Latin phrase meaning "Where are you going?"

Modern usage of the phrase refers to the strength, courage, and dedication of Saint Peter and his journey to spread the message of Jesus.

To most, "where are you going?" is just a question, to us it is a phrase that we are humbled by.
